# Live-animal shipping safety policy

## Temperature thresholds

- Minimum safe temperature: 30 F
- Maximum safe temperature: 85 F

## Decision rule

- If any stop is forecast outside the safe range, HOLD shipment.
- Include overnight lows and daytime highs.
- Check same-day conditions.
- Primary shipping service: FedEx Priority Overnight.

## Default outputs

- SAFE: all checked points remain within range
- HOLD: any checked point falls outside range
- REVIEW: route confidence or weather confidence is insufficient
